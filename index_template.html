Here, I will be sharing with you the work - IL and lab activities, I have completed during weeks 1-3. The topic I have chosen for my project is "AI-Enhanced UI Design."

The reason behind my topic is simple, throughout the last couple of months user interface design has changed a lot. The reason for the recent shift in user interface design is AI and the introduction of AI tools. Giants like Figma, Webflow, Adobe, and Framer are examples of design software that have successfully implemented AI tools. Many designers are concerned about the future of their career path due to this development.

My goal with this website is to help individuals in the fields of UX/UI design, web design, and graphic design by providing the latest trends, some tutorials, and information about the latest tools and novelties in the world of AI that could be beneficial for your future design projects. Anyone who is looking to enhance their design workflow is welcome. I am confident that you will find my findings helpful.

Enjoy the process!
