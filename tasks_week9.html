Week 9 IL Activity

Analysing Digital Video

Task 1: Analyse the use of video on the web

Once again, I have decided to work with the Flux Academy’s website, since it is a website similar to the one I’m working on.

What the Videos are Used For: The videos on the website are used primarily for educational purposes. They offer tutorials, tips, and insights into web design, user experience, and digital media. It is important to note that the target audience of Flux’s website and mine are quite alike. What is more, interviews with industry professionals, case studies, and reviews of design tools and software can also be found on the website.

Evidence of Video Used with Intent: Educational videos are commonly structured to enhance comprehension and retention by breaking down complex concepts into digestible segments. In this case, by using visual aids, and providing practical examples. Also, I find the real-world examples and step-by-step tutorials great for learning new concepts. This might be appealing to an even wider range of viewers.

Things to consider when creating content for my website:

Advantages: The dynamic way to learn that videos offer makes it easy for people with different learning styles. A key factor is the combination of visual and auditory elements since they offer a more engaging learning experience. For example, one can pause at any time, and take notes if necessary, or even go forward and back, if they missed something.

Disadvantages: The positive outcomes I just discussed depend entirely on the quality, presentation, and relevance to the audience. If done poorly, the video content might make viewers leave.

Suitability of Video Length: the ideal length of educational videos varies. However, they should be edited in a way that maintains viewer engagement, and at the same time provides enough information on the topic.

Combination of Video with Other Media: Often, even in the videos on the Flux Academy website, videos are complemented by other media like text and audio. This is a combination I am planning to add to the final version of my website.
